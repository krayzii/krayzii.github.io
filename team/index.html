D&D Dice

There are six main dice used in the D&D system, each with the corresponding number of sides as their name. This includes the D4, D6, D8, D10, D12, and D20. Most of your dice rolls revolve around the D20, but some, like damage rolls and hit dice, use others.

A classes hit die is the type you will use when rolling to recover or gain Hp. The higher the hit die, the tankier the class.

.

  • D6

    • Hit dice for the Wizard and Sorcerer classes

  • D8

    • Hit dice for the Bard, Cleric, Monk, Druid, Rogue, and Warlock classes

  • D10

    • Hit dice for the Ranger, Fighter, and Paladin classes

  • D12

    • Hit dice for Barbarian class

    Ability Scores

    The Ability Scores in D&D are the main stats you choose or roll for your character at the beginning of the game. These will help determine how strong or weak is your character's ability in this particular field.

    D&D Ability Scores explained by classes and tomatoes:

    .

  • STR

    • Strength is the ability to crush the tomato

    Main stat used by the Barbarian and Fighter

  • DEX

    • Dexterity is the ability to dodge tomato

    Main stat used by the Monk, Ranger, and Rogue classes

  • CON

    • Constitution is the ability to eat tomato

    Used by any and everybody to stay alive!

  • INT

    • Is knowing a tomato is a fruit

    Main stat used by the Wizard and Artificer classes

  • WIS

    • Wisdom is knowing NOT to put a tomato in fruit salad

    Main stat used by the Druid and Cleric classes

  • CHA

    • Charisma is being able to sell a tomato based fruit salad

    Main stat used by the Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, and Paladin classes
